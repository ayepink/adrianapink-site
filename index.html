About
The story

Engineer. Builder.
Community maker.

I'm Adriana — a Senior PM, yoga teacher, and writer based in New York City. I hold a Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering degree from NYU, which means I approach everything with the same rigor: whether I'm shipping a product feature, leading a vinyasa class, or writing about what it means to build a full, intentional life.

My career has taken me through IBM, The Walt Disney Company, Gastrograph AI, The Weather Company, and now Morgan & Morgan — where I lead Salesforce integrations for Medical Operations. Through all of it, I've stayed committed to the work that keeps me grounded: moving my body, building community, and writing honestly about the ride.

Doing it with passion. Staying resilient. Always.
